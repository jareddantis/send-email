How does it work?

This tool allows you to create mailto links (Wikipedia ↗), which are a special type of link that directs a user to an email address instead of a webpage.

Which link shortener are you using?

send-e.ml uses the fantastic shrtco.de ↗ link shortening API. Shortening your email template links through this app means that you agree to shrtcode's Terms of Service ↗

You're free to use any other link shortener you want! Just press Copy Link and paste the link in your preferred link shortener.

Is my data being stored?

Link generation is performed completely on your device, all through JavaScript. None of your data is transmitted remotely from this app, until you press the Shorten Link button.

send-e.ml itself does not use cookies.
